[1] Universal Robots, *UR3 User Manual*, versión 5.x. Odense, Dinamarca: Universal Robots A/S, 2023. [En línea]. Disponible en: <https://www.universal-robots.com/manuals>

[3] Rockwell Automation, *Micro850 Programmable Controller User Manual*, Publication 2080-UM002. Milwaukee, WI: Rockwell Automation, 2023.
